About Vikiran
The word "Vikiran" symbolizes a beam of light and hope which will enlighten your path to display innumerable opportunities in different aspects where you can excel in life. This fest will provide you a platform to showcase your talent in areas of life skills, arts and technical work where you can participate and learn with games and activities. This fest is anually hosted by Delhi Technical Campus.
